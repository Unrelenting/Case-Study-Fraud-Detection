Fraud Detection

By Elham, Jay, Joseph, Shige, and Matthew

Overview

Our application is able to predict the percent chance that an event is fraudulent and categorize the level of risk that these events are actually fraudulent. It also calculates the total revenue for each event, so it is easy to see the possible cost of refunding customers for each event if they are fraudulent, so you can make informed business decisions backed by statistical evidence. You can see the most current analysis done on information sent to our servers.

We will soon be implementing ways so you can customize the parameters used in our models, so that you can edit risk level thresholds as well as a few other parameters on our website. We will also look at creating summaries of the data as a lot of information is being processed, and you will be able to get those summaries based on intervals at your choosing.
